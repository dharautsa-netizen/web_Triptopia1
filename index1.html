Popular Tourist Spots In India
Visit Kolkata In This Durga Puja

Kolkata's colonial architecture, a testament to its role as the British colonial capital, features British influence through neoclassical, Gothic, and Victorian styles. Iconic buildings like the Victoria Memorial, Writers' Building, Howrah Bridge, St. Paul's Cathedral, Indian Museum etc.

Explore Jaipur In This Winter

Udaipur is special for its beautiful City Palaces, scenic lakes like Lake Pichola, and its romantic ambiance, earning it nicknames like the "White City" and the "Venice of the East".

The Andaman and Nicobar Islands is a union territory of India

The territory includes the famous Barren Island, which is home to India's only active volcano. The islands offer numerous attractions, including diverse coral reefs, vibrant marine life, and opportunities for adventure sports, making them a popular holiday and honeymoon destination.

International Holiday Pacakages

Singapore

A non-stop, five-hour flight to Singapore from Bengaluru will cost you between INR 15,000 and INR 17,000 depending upon the season and airlines. The sunny little island can make a perfect destination for a winter vacation, as July to December is the best time to experience its winters. Check out the rooftop bars in the city like Levant, 1-Arden, etc. that offer the best views of the skyline. Depending on the class, the cost of accommodation starts from INR 4,000.

Krabi, Thailand

One of the popular honeymoon and beach destinations, this is an excellent budget getaway for winters. Since Krabi experiences rainfall till October, the months from November to March are the perfect time to visit this place. The flights from India start at INR 25,000. One can get a decent accommodation for as low as INR 3,000. Experience the scenic, pristine beaches and the thumping nightlife of Krabi.

Book Now

Dubai, UAE

The best time to visit this tourist jewel is from October to April. The flight tickets to Dubai begin from INR 13,000, and one can get fairly reasonable accommodation starting from INR 5,000. Apart from exploring the Dubai Marina and Burj Khalifa, one can also enjoy Dubai’s desert safaris when the heat is not scorching. One can save money on taxis by exploring the city on the recently invested rental bike scheme.

Book Now

Cambodia

Create an itinerary to visit the colossal temple city of Cambodia, Angkor Wat or the nature-filled reprieve of Ratanakiri this winter. Being one of the cheapest foreign destinations from India, the best time to visit Cambodia is from November to April. The cost of air tickets starts from INR 25,000. The approximate cost of taking a week-long trip to Cambodia will begin from INR 45,000.

Book Now

Bhutan

October to December is the ideal time to visit Bhutan. If you wish to see the rare black-necked cranes, you can visit Phobjikha Valley from November to mid-February. Offering scenic, pristine wonders, the country is also called the ‘Land of the Thunder Dragons’. A week-long trip to Bhutan, including airfare, will cost you around INR 35,000.

Book Now

Istanbul, Turkey

Although Istanbul is the tourist gem of Turkey, but its real capital, Ankara, is absolutely worth a visit. The cost of flights to Istanbul starts from INR 25,000. The best time to visit is from September to December. You can also take a road trip in Turkey to explore its hidden gems like Alacati, the natural pools of Pamukkale, the Nemrut Dagi Mountain, Cappadocia or the Sumela Monastery in Macka.
